MY PROYECTS

Design of grinder and priting machine for the production of cacao

The purpose of this project was principally to create a machine with which to extract the essential oil of cocoa through heating and the crushing of the cocoa beans. This project had a focus on housewives, because thanks to this machine extraction of essential oils is easier for them. We had an inclination to extract cocoa oil for its properties and benefits in addition to the various utilities has in the industrial environment. Order to obtain the heating of the machine had to buy heat resistance, because only then would be achieved cacao extract oil. After we have bought resistance, it was tested whether oil could be extracted. In the presentation of projects, the oil obtained and the machine developed by project members was shown.

Neutral pH soap based on natural products

The purpose of this project was to obtain a soap with neutral pH because the pH of each person is different ranges between 4.5 and 5.9 pH. With a neutral pH soap can prevent that not occur skin lacerations or checkouts, because to the obtain a neutral pH can use it to not produce a more alkaline pH in the skin. To carry out this experiment, we investigated certain concepts as saponification which is a chemical process between a fatty acid and an alkali, so it results in obtaining a soap. The saponification method consisted in boiling fat and then slowly add sodium hydroxide because thanks to this composite a solid soap is obtained but if it had used potassium hydroxide would have obtained a liquid soap.
